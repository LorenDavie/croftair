A fantasy world, with Creative Commons licensing.

Welcome to Croftair, a project I started in 1984. Originally conceived as a background for fantasy roleplaying games, I found myself continuing to work on Croftair as a hobby, long after I stopped playing those games. I found it a great way to relax, and take my mind off the troubles of the day.

After some consideration, I've decided I probably won't write the next great fantasy novel, so instead I though I would try to share my project with the world. You are free to use this content in stories, games, and so forth, as per the terms of the license.

Please enjoy. I can be reached on Twitter at @LorenDavie.
